Magazine Layout

Objective

Design the cover with masthead and two separate spreads for the table of contents and article layout for a magazine.

Ascend is a fictitious magazine whose primary audience is the traveling artist. This edition revolves around art in New York with the featured artist, Alyssa Monks.

Museum Brochure

Objective

Collect content and imagery to design a promotional brochure for a museum exhibit. Determine the layout based on one's chosen page size.

City Dreams is an exhibition that was held at MOMA in New York. I wanted to show the bold and intricate quality of Bodys Isek Kingelez's creations against a white background to mimic the look of the exhibiton.

Book Jacket Trilogy

Objective

Create imagery and compose the layout for three different episodes of WNYC’s RadioLab.

My designs are intended to provoke a sense of uneasiness by placing viewer in a situation that encourages them to reflect on their own definition of my chosen topics.

Morality–I designed an image to cause discomfort to make the viewer question their concept of morality.

Stress–I chose to depict the volatile nature of stress while presenting the viewer with a situation that can prolong their stress depending on how they choose to act or change their actions.

Help–The podcast discusses different methods people use to overcome mental obstacles. I put the viewer in a position of vulnerability to reflect the struggle that one undertakes to reach their goal by pulling themselves out.
